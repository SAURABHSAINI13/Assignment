Travel Bucket List

Explore the most breathtaking destinations around the world that I dream of visiting one day. From natural wonders to vibrant cities, here are my top picks:

  1. Kyoto, Japan

    Kyoto is famous for its classical Buddhist temples, beautiful gardens, imperial palaces, and traditional wooden houses. It offers a deep dive into Japanese culture and heritage.

    Visit Japan Travel - Kyoto

  2. Santorini, Greece

    Famous for its stunning sunsets, white-washed buildings with blue domes, and breathtaking views of the Aegean Sea. Santorini is a dreamlike island ideal for relaxation and picturesque moments.

    Discover Santorini on Visit Greece

  3. Banff National Park, Canada

    A paradise for nature lovers, Banff features crystal-clear lakes, snow-capped mountains, and endless trails for hiking and wildlife spotting. Lake Louise and the surrounding Rockies make it a must-see.

    Explore Banff on Parks Canada
